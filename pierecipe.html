Lemon Meringue Pie

  • One 9-inch pie
  • 30 min-Prep time
  • 10 min-Cook time
  • 40 min-Total
  • 8 Servings

    • Ingredients

    • 1 cup white sugar
    • 2 tbsp all-purpouse flour
    • 3 tbsp cornstarch
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1 1/2 cups water
    • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 4 egg yolks, beaten
    • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
    • 4 egg whites
    • 6 tbsp white sugar

      1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
